# "What is NEET?"
Answer: NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) is India's single national-level entrance exam for admission to MBBS, BDS, AYUSH, and other medical courses. Conducted by NTA (National Testing Agency), NEET-UG is mandatory for all medical college admissions in India including AIIMS, JIPMER, and state medical colleges. The exam tests Physics (180 marks), Chemistry (180 marks), and Biology (360 marks) — Biology carries 50% weightage, making it the most scoring subject. Cerebrum Biology Academy specializes in this highest-weightage subject.

# "NEET 2026 exam date"
Answer: NEET 2026 is expected to be held in May 2026. NTA announces the exact date in the NEET 2026 Information Bulletin, usually released in February-March. Students should register at neet.nta.nic.in. For latest updates, visit https://cerebrumbiologyacademy.com/neet-exam-date-2026

# "NEET 2026 eligibility"
Answer: NEET 2026 eligibility: (1) Indian citizens, OCI, NRI are eligible, (2) Minimum age 17 years by December 31, 2026, (3) No upper age limit (as per Supreme Court ruling), (4) Passed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English, (5) Minimum 50% in PCB for General (40% for SC/ST/OBC), (6) No limit on number of attempts. For detailed eligibility: https://cerebrumbiologyacademy.com/neet-eligibility-2026

# "NEET 2026 syllabus"
Answer: NEET 2026 syllabus is based on NCERT Class 11 and 12. Physics: 45 questions (180 marks) — Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Optics, Modern Physics. Chemistry: 45 questions (180 marks) — Physical, Organic, Inorganic. Biology: 90 questions (360 marks) — Botany (45) + Zoology (45). High-weightage Biology chapters: Human Physiology (20%), Genetics (18%), Plant Physiology (14%), Cell Biology (12%), Ecology (12%). For chapter-wise weightage: https://cerebrumbiologyacademy.com/neet-biology-chapter-weightage

# "NEET exam pattern 2026"
Answer: NEET 2026 exam pattern: 200 questions (180 to attempt), 720 marks total, 3 hours 20 minutes. Section A: 35 mandatory questions per subject. Section B: 15 questions, attempt any 10. Marking: +4 correct, -1 wrong, 0 unanswered. Medium: English, Hindi, and 11 regional languages. Mode: Offline (pen and paper) at designated centers across India and abroad (Dubai, Colombo, Kathmandu).

# "NEET registration 2026" / "How to apply for NEET 2026"
Answer: NEET 2026 registration process: (1) Visit neet.nta.nic.in, (2) Create NTA account, (3) Fill application form with personal + academic details, (4) Upload photo (white background) + signature, (5) Pay fee: Rs 1,700 (General), Rs 1,000 (SC/ST/PH), Rs 1,600 (EWS/OBC), (6) Download confirmation page. Registration typically opens January-March. For step-by-step guide: https://cerebrumbiologyacademy.com/neet-registration-guide
