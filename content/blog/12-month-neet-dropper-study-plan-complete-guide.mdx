# 12-Month NEET Dropper Study Plan

This is your roadmap from Day 1 of drop year to NEET exam day. Follow it precisely for maximum results.

---

## Plan Overview

### The 4-Phase Strategy

| Phase | Months | Duration | Focus |
|-------|--------|----------|-------|
| **Foundation** | 1-3 | 12 weeks | Basics + Class 11 revision |
| **Building** | 4-7 | 16 weeks | Complete syllabus + practice |
| **Practice** | 8-10 | 12 weeks | Mocks + weak areas |
| **Peak** | 11-12 | 8 weeks | Revision + exam readiness |

### Daily Time Distribution

| Subject | Hours | Percentage |
|---------|-------|------------|
| Biology | 3.5-4 hrs | 40% |
| Physics | 2-2.5 hrs | 25% |
| Chemistry | 2-2.5 hrs | 25% |
| Revision/Tests | 1 hr | 10% |
| **Total** | **8-10 hrs** | 100% |

## Subject-Wise Strategy Throughout

### Biology (40% Time Allocation)

**Monthly NCERT Reading Schedule:**
| Month | NCERT Read # | Focus |
|-------|--------------|-------|
| 1-2 | Read 1 | Understanding |
| 3-4 | Read 2 | Highlighting |
| 5-6 | Read 3 | MCQ connection |
| 7-8 | Read 4 | Line-by-line |
| 9-10 | Read 5 | Exam focus |
| 11-12 | Read 6 | Final revision |

**Diagram Practice Schedule:**
- Month 1-6: Learn all diagrams
- Month 7-9: Draw from memory weekly
- Month 10-12: Quick diagram revision daily

### Physics (25% Time Allocation)

**Chapter Priority:**
| Priority | Chapters | Time Allocation |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| High | Mechanics, Modern Physics | 40% |
| Medium | Optics, Electromagnetism | 35% |
| Low | Thermodynamics, Waves | 25% |

**Formula Mastery:**
- Write all formulas weekly
- Derivations: Understand, don't memorize
- Numerical practice: 20 problems/day

### Chemistry (25% Time Allocation)

**Section Distribution:**
| Section | Time | Strategy |
|---------|------|----------|
| Physical | 30% | Formula + numericals |
| Organic | 40% | Reactions + mechanisms |
| Inorganic | 30% | NCERT only |

**Organic Chemistry Approach:**
- Months 1-4: Understand mechanisms
- Months 5-8: Memorize reactions
- Months 9-12: Quick revision sheets
