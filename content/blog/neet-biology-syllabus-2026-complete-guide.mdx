# NEET Biology Syllabus 2026: Complete Official Guide

The NEET 2026 Biology syllabus is based on Class 11 and Class 12 NCERT textbooks. With 90 questions worth 360 marks, Biology is the most scoring subject in NEET. This comprehensive guide covers every chapter, topic, and sub-topic you need to study.

## NEET 2026 Biology Overview

| Aspect          | Details                       |
| --------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Total Questions | 90 (45 Botany + 45 Zoology)   |
| Total Marks     | 360                           |
| Marking Scheme  | +4 for correct, -1 for wrong  |
| Question Source | Class 11 & 12 NCERT Biology   |
| Exam Authority  | NTA (National Testing Agency) |
| Exam Date 2026  | May 4, 2026 (Expected)        |

## NEET 2026 Biology Preparation Tips

### 1. Master NCERT First

- Read every line, example, and diagram
- 95% of NEET questions come from NCERT
- Make notes while reading

### 2. Focus on High-Weightage Units

- Human Physiology: 20-24% questions
- Genetics: 17-20% questions
- Ecology: 11-13% questions
- Reproduction: 11-13% questions

### 3. Practice Previous Year Questions

- Solve last 10 years NEET papers
- Identify repeated concepts
- Analyze your weak areas

### 4. Revision Strategy

- Daily diagram practice (5 diagrams minimum)
- Weekly chapter revision
- Monthly full-syllabus test

### 5. Important Resources

- NCERT Class 11 & 12 Biology
- NCERT Exemplar
- Previous year NEET papers
- Mock tests from reliable sources

Yes, NTA has not announced any changes in the NEET 2026 Biology syllabus. It remains based on Class 11 and 12 NCERT.

### How many chapters are in NEET Biology?

NEET Biology covers 38 chapters - 22 from Class 11 and 16 from Class 12 NCERT.

### Which class has more weightage in NEET Biology?

Class 12 has slightly higher weightage (55%) compared to Class 11 (45%), but both are equally important.

### Can I score 360 in NEET Biology?

Yes, with thorough NCERT reading, consistent practice, and strategic revision, scoring 340+ is achievable.
