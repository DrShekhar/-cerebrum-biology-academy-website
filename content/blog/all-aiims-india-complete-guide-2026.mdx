## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is AIIMS Delhi really worth the hype?

Yes. AIIMS Delhi offers unmatched clinical exposure (10,000+ patients daily), research opportunities, faculty quality, and alumni network. The training here is considered the best in India.

### Should I choose a newer AIIMS or an older state GMC?

AIIMS (even newer ones) generally offer better infrastructure, research opportunities, and the AIIMS brand advantage for PG admissions. Choose AIIMS over most state GMCs.

### Do newer AIIMS have good faculty?

Yes. Many faculty members in newer AIIMS are transfers from AIIMS Delhi or trained at AIIMS Delhi. Quality is good and improving.

### Is hostel mandatory at AIIMS?

Yes, for the first year. After that, students can choose to stay outside, though most prefer hostel due to affordability and community.

### What is the selection ratio for AIIMS Delhi?

Less than 1 in 2 lakh general category candidates get AIIMS Delhi - more competitive than Ivy League schools.
