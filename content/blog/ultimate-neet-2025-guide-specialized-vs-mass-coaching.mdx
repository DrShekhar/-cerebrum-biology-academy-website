## Understanding NEET: The Complete Picture

### What is NEET?

The **National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET-UG)** is India's single largest medical entrance examination, conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA). It's your gateway to:

- **MBBS** courses across all government and private medical colleges
- **BDS** (Dental) courses
- **BAMS, BHMS, BUMS** (AYUSH courses)
- **BSc Nursing** in many institutions
- **Veterinary** courses (NEET is required in some states)

### NEET 2025 Exam Pattern

| Section | Questions | Marks | Time |
|---------|-----------|-------|------|
| Physics | 50 (45 to attempt) | 180 | - |
| Chemistry | 50 (45 to attempt) | 180 | - |

| **Total** | **200 (180 to attempt)** | **720** | **3 hours 20 min** |
