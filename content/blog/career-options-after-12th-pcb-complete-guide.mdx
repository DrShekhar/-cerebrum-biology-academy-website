# Career Options After 12th PCB: The Complete 2025 Guide

Gone are the days when PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) meant only MBBS or nothing. In 2025, PCB students have **50+ exciting career paths** with salaries ranging from ₹3 LPA to ₹30+ LPA. This comprehensive guide covers every option with entrance exam details, preparation strategies, and college information.

---

## Quick Overview: Career Categories

| Category | NEET Required? | Entry Salary | Top Salary | Key Entrance Exams |
|----------|----------------|--------------|------------|-------------------|
| Medical (MBBS/BDS) | Yes | ₹8-15 LPA | ₹50+ LPA | NEET-UG |
| Alternative Medicine | Yes | ₹4-8 LPA | ₹20+ LPA | NEET-UG |
| Allied Health Sciences | No | ₹3-6 LPA | ₹15+ LPA | State exams/Merit |

## Part 1: Allied Health Sciences (Without NEET)

These healthcare careers offer **92% placement rate** within 6 months of graduation and are in high demand globally.

### B.Sc. Nursing

The most sought-after allied health career with international demand.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Duration** | 4 years |
| **Eligibility** | 10+2 with PCB, minimum 45% (40% for reserved) |
| **Entry Salary** | ₹3-6 LPA (India), ₹25-50 LPA (Abroad) |
| **Growth** | 17-18% projected growth by 2027 |

#### Entrance Exams for B.Sc. Nursing

| Exam | Conducting Body | Colleges | Exam Date 2025 |
|------|-----------------|----------|----------------|
| **AIIMS Nursing** | AIIMS | AIIMS institutes | June 2025 |
| **JIPMER Nursing** | JIPMER | JIPMER Puducherry | May 2025 |
| **BHU Nursing** | BHU | Banaras Hindu University | May 2025 |
| **PGIMER Nursing** | PGIMER | PGIMER Chandigarh | June 2025 |
| **State B.Sc. Nursing** | State Boards | Government colleges | April-June 2025 |

**Syllabus Focus:**
- Physics: 25%
- Chemistry: 25%
- Biology: 40%
- English/General Knowledge: 10%

**Cutoff Range:**
- AIIMS Nursing: 80-90 percentile
- Government Colleges: 70-85%
- Private Colleges: 50-65%
