   - "Which hormone regulates blood calcium?" → Parathyroid hormone

### 2. Genetics & Molecular Biology (14-16% of Biology)

**Most Repeated Topics:**

| Topic | Times in 10 Years | Probability 2026 |
|-------|-------------------|------------------|
| Mendelian ratios | 10/10 | 100% |
| DNA replication enzymes | 9/10 | 95% |
| Genetic code properties | 9/10 | 95% |
| Chromosomal disorders | 10/10 | 100% |
| Transcription | 8/10 | 90% |
| Pedigree analysis | 8/10 | 90% |

**Sample Repeated Questions:**

1. **Mendel's Laws (Asked every year):**
   - "9:3:3:1 ratio indicates" → Independent assortment
   - "Test cross ratio for dihybrid" → 1:1:1:1
   - "Law of segregation is also called" → Law of purity of gametes

2. **DNA Replication:**
   - "Okazaki fragments are formed on" → Lagging strand
   - "Leading strand is synthesized" → Continuously, 5' to 3'
   - "DNA polymerase adds nucleotides at" → 3' end

3. **Genetic Disorders (Asked every year):**
   - "47, XXY karyotype" → Klinefelter syndrome
   - "Trisomy 21" → Down syndrome
   - "XO condition" → Turner syndrome

**Must-Know Genetic Ratios:**

| Cross Type | Ratio | Condition |
|------------|-------|-----------|
| Monohybrid | 3:1 | Complete dominance |
| Dihybrid | 9:3:3:1 | Independent assortment |
| Test cross (mono) | 1:1 | Heterozygous parent |
| Test cross (di) | 1:1:1:1 | Double heterozygous |
| Incomplete dominance | 1:2:1 | Blending phenotype |
| Codominance | 1:2:1 | Both expressed |

## Pattern Analysis by Question Type

### Assertion-Reason Questions

**Common patterns in Biology A-R:**

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
