## Revision Techniques

### Active Recall Method

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Close book |
| 2 | Write down everything you remember |
| 3 | Check with NCERT |
| 4 | Focus on missed points |
| 5 | Repeat next day |

### Spaced Repetition Schedule

| Topic Revised | Repeat After |
|---------------|--------------|
| Day 1 | Day 3, Day 7, Day 15 |
| Day 2 | Day 4, Day 8, Day 16 |
| And so on... | ... |

### Pomodoro Technique

| Session | Duration |
|---------|----------|
| Study | 45 minutes |
| Break | 10 minutes |
| After 4 sessions | 30 min break |

## Common Mistakes in Last 30 Days

### What NOT to Do

| Mistake | Why Bad | Solution |
|---------|---------|----------|
| Starting new topics | No time for mastery | Revise what you know |
| Skipping mocks | No exam practice | 15 mocks minimum |
| All-nighters | Poor retention | 7+ hours sleep |
| New books | Different language | Stick to NCERT |
| Ignoring weak areas | Predictable marks loss | Target improvement |

### What TO Do

| Action | Benefit |
|--------|---------|
| Morning NCERT reading | Fresh mind retention |
| Daily PYQ practice | Pattern recognition |
| Alternate day mocks | Exam simulation |
| Early sleep | Memory consolidation |
| Light exercise | Stress reduction |
