## Preparation Strategy for NEET 2026

### Based on 2025 Analysis

**Phase 1 (Months 1-4): Foundation**
- Complete NCERT Class 11 & 12
- Focus on understanding concepts
- Make notes of important diagrams
- Cover high-weightage chapters first

**Phase 2 (Months 5-8): Deep Learning**

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Was NEET 2025 Biology paper tough?

NEET 2025 Biology paper was moderate in difficulty - easier than 2024 but required thorough NCERT preparation. Students with strong fundamentals found it manageable.

### Which chapters had most questions in NEET 2025?

Human Physiology (15 questions), Genetics (14 questions), and Plant Reproduction (5 questions) had the maximum representation.

### Is NCERT enough based on NEET 2025 pattern?

Yes, 85% questions were directly from NCERT. For remaining 15%, understanding concepts deeply and PYQ practice is sufficient.

### What score is expected for 680+ in NEET 2025?

For 680+, you need approximately 340+ in Biology. This requires attempting 85+ questions with 90%+ accuracy.

### How should I prepare for NEET 2026 based on this analysis?

Focus on Human Physiology, Genetics, and Reproduction. Read NCERT line by line, practice diagrams, and solve chapter-wise PYQs.
