## Assertion-Reason Expected Patterns

### Biology A-R Format

Expect 8-10 A-R questions in Biology. Common patterns:

1. **Both True, Reason Correct Explanation:**
   - A: Mitochondria have their own DNA
   - R: Mitochondria are semi-autonomous organelles

2. **Both True, Reason NOT Correct Explanation:**
   - A: Insulin controls blood sugar
   - R: Insulin is secreted by beta cells of islets

3. **A True, R False:**
   - A: O-negative is universal donor
   - R: O-negative has A and B antibodies

### Practice A-R Logic

| Pattern | What to Check |
|---------|---------------|
| Both A and R true | Does R actually explain A? |
| A true, R false | Is the reason factually wrong? |
| A false | Check the assertion first |
