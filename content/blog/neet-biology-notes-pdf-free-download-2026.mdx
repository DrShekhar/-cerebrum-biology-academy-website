## Chapter-wise NEET Biology Notes

### Unit 1: Diversity of Living Organisms

#### Chapter 1: The Living World

**Key Topics:**
- Characteristics of living organisms
- Taxonomic hierarchy
- Binomial nomenclature
- Taxonomic aids

**Important Points:**
- Living organisms are self-replicating, evolving, and self-regulating
- Metabolism is the defining feature of all living organisms
- Taxonomic hierarchy: Kingdom → Phylum → Class → Order → Family → Genus → Species
- Mnemonic: **K**ing **P**hillip **C**ame **O**ver **F**or **G**ood **S**oup

**NEET Focus:** 2-3 questions expected, mostly factual

---

#### Chapter 2: Biological Classification

**Key Topics:**
- Five Kingdom Classification (Whittaker)
- Characteristics of each kingdom
- Viruses, Viroids, Lichens

**Important Tables:**

| Kingdom | Cell Type | Cell Wall | Nutrition |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Monera | Prokaryotic | Present (peptidoglycan) | Autotrophic/Heterotrophic |
| Protista | Eukaryotic | Present/Absent | Autotrophic/Heterotrophic |
| Fungi | Eukaryotic | Present (chitin) | Heterotrophic |
| Plantae | Eukaryotic | Present (cellulose) | Autotrophic |
| Animalia | Eukaryotic | Absent | Heterotrophic |

**NEET Focus:** 3-4 questions expected

---

#### Chapter 3: Plant Kingdom

**Key Topics:**
- Classification of plants
- Algae, Bryophytes, Pteridophytes
- Gymnosperms, Angiosperms
- Life cycles and alternation of generations

**Important Comparisons:**

| Feature | Bryophytes | Pteridophytes | Gymnosperms |
|---------|------------|---------------|-------------|
| Vascular tissue | Absent | Present | Present |
| Dominant phase | Gametophyte | Sporophyte | Sporophyte |
| Seeds | Absent | Absent | Present (naked) |

**NEET Focus:** 2-3 questions expected

---

#### Chapter 4: Animal Kingdom

**Key Topics:**
- Basis of classification
- Non-chordates (Porifera to Echinodermata)
- Chordates (Protochordata to Mammalia)

**Mnemonic for Phyla:**
**P**oor **C**ute **P**latypus **A**te **M**y **E**ntire **A**pple **C**ake
(Porifera, Cnidaria, Platyhelminthes, Aschelminthes, Mollusca, Echinodermata, Arthropoda, Chordata)

**NEET Focus:** 4-5 questions expected (high weightage chapter)

---

### Unit 2: Cell Structure and Function

#### Chapter 5: Cell - The Unit of Life

**Key Topics:**
- Cell theory
- Prokaryotic vs Eukaryotic cells
- Cell organelles and their functions
- Endomembrane system

**Important Diagram Points:**
- Learn labeled diagrams of: Plant cell, Animal cell, Mitochondria, Chloroplast, Nucleus
- Fluid mosaic model of plasma membrane

**NEET Focus:** 3-4 questions expected

---

#### Chapter 6: Biomolecules

**Key Topics:**
- Carbohydrates, Proteins, Lipids, Nucleic acids
- Enzymes and their properties
- Metabolic pathways

**Important Facts:**
- Proteins are polymers of amino acids (20 types)
- DNA: Deoxyribose sugar + Phosphate + Nitrogenous bases (A, T, G, C)
- RNA: Ribose sugar + Phosphate + Nitrogenous bases (A, U, G, C)

**NEET Focus:** 3-4 questions expected

---

#### Chapter 7: Cell Cycle and Cell Division

**Key Topics:**
- Cell cycle phases (G1, S, G2, M)
- Mitosis and Meiosis
- Significance of meiosis

**Key Differences:**

| Feature | Mitosis | Meiosis |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Division type | Equational | Reductional |
| Daughter cells | 2 (diploid) | 4 (haploid) |
| Crossing over | No | Yes (Prophase I) |
| Occurs in | Somatic cells | Germ cells |

**NEET Focus:** 3-4 questions expected

---

### Unit 3: Human Physiology (HIGH WEIGHTAGE)

#### Chapter 8: Digestion and Absorption

**Key Topics:**
- Human digestive system
- Digestive enzymes and their actions
- Absorption of digested products
- Disorders of digestive system

**Important Enzymes Table:**

| Organ | Enzyme | Substrate | Product |
|-------|--------|-----------|---------|
| Mouth | Salivary amylase | Starch | Maltose |
| Stomach | Pepsin | Proteins | Peptones |
| Pancreas | Trypsin | Proteins | Peptides |
| Pancreas | Lipase | Fats | Fatty acids + Glycerol |
| Intestine | Lactase | Lactose | Glucose + Galactose |

**NEET Focus:** 3-4 questions expected

---

#### Chapter 9: Breathing and Exchange of Gases

**Key Topics:**
- Respiratory organs
- Mechanism of breathing
- Exchange of gases
- Transport of gases
- Respiratory disorders

**Important Values:**
- Tidal Volume: 500 mL
- Inspiratory Reserve Volume: 2500-3000 mL
- Expiratory Reserve Volume: 1000-1100 mL
- Residual Volume: 1100-1200 mL
- Vital Capacity: 3400-4800 mL

**NEET Focus:** 2-3 questions expected

---

#### Chapter 10: Body Fluids and Circulation

**Key Topics:**
- Blood composition and functions
- Blood groups
- Coagulation of blood
- Human heart structure
- Cardiac cycle
- ECG

**Important Facts:**
- Blood groups: A, B, AB, O (based on antigens)
- Universal donor: O negative
- Universal recipient: AB positive
- Cardiac output = Stroke volume × Heart rate

**NEET Focus:** 4-5 questions expected (high yield)

---

#### Chapter 11: Excretory Products and Their Elimination

**Key Topics:**
- Modes of excretion
- Human excretory system
- Urine formation
- Regulation of kidney function
- Disorders

**Key Process: Urine Formation**
1. Glomerular filtration (125 mL/min)
2. Tubular reabsorption (124 mL/min reabsorbed)
3. Tubular secretion
4. Final urine: ~1.5 L/day

**NEET Focus:** 3-4 questions expected

---

#### Chapter 12: Locomotion and Movement

**Key Topics:**
- Types of movement
- Muscle structure and contraction
- Skeletal system
- Joints
- Disorders

**Sliding Filament Theory Key Points:**
- Actin (thin filaments) slide over myosin (thick filaments)
- Requires Ca²⁺ and ATP
- H-zone and I-band decrease during contraction

**NEET Focus:** 2-3 questions expected
