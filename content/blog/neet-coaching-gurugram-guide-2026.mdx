## What Makes NEET Coaching in Gurugram Different

### The Affluence Factor

Gurugram families often have resources for the best possible coaching, but money alone does not guarantee results. We have seen students who attended premium 1-on-1 tutoring for years without meaningful score improvement because the methodology was wrong. NEET Biology requires:

- Systematic NCERT coverage (not fancy supplementary material)
- Thousands of MCQ practice questions
- Regular full-length mock tests under exam conditions
- Targeted weak-area improvement

### IB and IGCSE Students

A significant number of Gurugram students come from IB or IGCSE backgrounds. Their Biology curriculum differs substantially from CBSE, which means:

- They need additional time for NCERT-based topics not covered in IB/IGCSE
- Certain chapters (Plant Physiology, Ecology in NEET context) require building from scratch
- The MCQ format itself requires specific training — IB assessments are largely descriptive

At Cerebrum, we have a specific onboarding process for IB/IGCSE students that bridges curriculum gaps before the main NEET program begins.

## How to Evaluate Any Gurugram NEET Institute

Before enrolling anywhere, ask these questions:

1. **What is the actual batch size?** (Not "up to" — the real number)
2. **Who teaches the classes?** (Meet the faculty, not just the marketing team)
3. **What are the verifiable results?** (Student names, scores, college admissions)
4. **Is the study material NCERT-aligned?** (Avoid institutes that push proprietary material over NCERT)
5. **What happens when my child misses a class?** (Makeup policy matters)
6. **Are mock tests included or charged separately?**
7. **Can I attend a demo class before paying?**
