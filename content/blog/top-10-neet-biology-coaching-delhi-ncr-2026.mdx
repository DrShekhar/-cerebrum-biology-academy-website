### 3. Biology Specialization

Biology is **50% of NEET marks** (180/360). Yet at multi-subject institutes:

- Physics gets 40% of class time (most complex formulas)
- Chemistry gets 35% of class time (organic reactions need practice)
- **Biology gets only 25% of class time** (despite being 50% of marks)

**Biology-specialized institutes** spend 100% of their time on the highest-weightage subject. This matters enormously for students who are strong in PCM but need biology mastery.

Ask every institute: **"What percentage of YOUR students qualified NEET last year?"**

- Big brands will deflect: _"We had 50 students in AIR 1-100!"_
- Ask again: _"Out of how many total students? What's the qualification rate?"_

Most big brands have 40-60% qualification rates. Top specialized institutes achieve 90-98%.

### 5. Individualized Mentorship

Does your child get:

- ✅ Weekly 1-on-1 performance reviews?
- ✅ Personalized study plans based on weak chapters?
- ✅ Direct WhatsApp access to faculty for doubts?
- ✅ Parent-teacher meetings every month?

At big brands with 100+ student batches, this level of attention is structurally impossible.

## Making Your Decision: A Parent's Checklist

Before you pay ₹1.5L-₹2.5L to a big brand, ask yourself:

### Questions to Ask Every Institute

1. **Batch Size**
   - ❓ "How many students will be in my child's batch?"
   - 🚩 Red flag: 80+ students
   - ✅ Good answer: 10-30 students

2. **Faculty Credentials**
   - ❓ "Can I meet the biology faculty who will teach my child?"
   - ❓ "What is their NEET/AIIMS/JIPMER rank and medical background?"
   - 🚩 Red flag: "Our faculty are experienced" (vague answer)
   - ✅ Good answer: Specific credentials (AIIMS alumni, MBBS, etc.)

3. **Success Rate**
   - ❓ "What percentage of YOUR students qualified NEET last year?"
   - 🚩 Red flag: "We had 50 top rankers!" (deflection)
   - ✅ Good answer: "85% of our students qualified" (specific percentage)

4. **Personal Attention**
   - ❓ "Will my child get 1-on-1 mentorship? How often?"
   - ❓ "Can I WhatsApp doubts directly to faculty?"
   - 🚩 Red flag: "We have doubt counters" or "Email your doubts"
   - ✅ Good answer: "Weekly 1-on-1 sessions + direct WhatsApp access"

5. **Biology Focus**
   - ❓ "What percentage of class time is spent on biology?"
   - ❓ "Is your faculty specialized in NEET biology or general PCM?"
   - 🚩 Red flag: "We cover all subjects equally"
   - ✅ Good answer: "We focus 100% on biology" or "50%+ time on biology"

### Visit Multiple Institutes

Don't decide based on marketing materials alone:

- ✅ Attend demo classes at 3-4 institutes
- ✅ Meet the actual faculty (not just counselors)
- ✅ Talk to current students and parents
- ✅ Check batch size in person (don't trust brochures)
- ✅ Ask for last year's batch results (verifiable, not cherry-picked)
