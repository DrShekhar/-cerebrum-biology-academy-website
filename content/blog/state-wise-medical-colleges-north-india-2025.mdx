## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Which state has the best private medical colleges in North India?

**For quality**: Delhi NCR (Hamdard, Sharda, Santosh) and Punjab (DMC Ludhiana)
**For affordability**: Uttar Pradesh (Era, TMMC, Hind)
**For value**: Rajasthan (Geetanjali, Pacific)

### 2. Can I apply to multiple state counsellings?

You can apply to **AIQ (All India Quota)** counselling regardless of domicile. For **State Quota**, you need respective state's domicile certificate.

### 3. What's the total cost difference between UP and Delhi NCR colleges?

| Aspect | UP (Mid-tier) | Delhi NCR | Difference |
|--------|---------------|-----------|------------|
| Tuition (5 yrs) | ‚Çπ75 Lakhs | ‚Çπ1.05 Crores | +‚Çπ30 Lakhs |
| Hostel (5 yrs) | ‚Çπ5 Lakhs | ‚Çπ8 Lakhs | +‚Çπ3 Lakhs |
| Living Expenses | ‚Çπ5 Lakhs | ‚Çπ8 Lakhs | +‚Çπ3 Lakhs |
| **Total** | ‚Çπ85 Lakhs | ‚Çπ1.21 Crores | +‚Çπ36 Lakhs |

### 4. Which colleges have the best hospital exposure?

| Hospital Beds | Colleges |
|---------------|----------|
| **1000+ beds** | DMC Ludhiana, Subharti Meerut, TMMC Moradabad, Era Lucknow |
| **800+ beds** | Sharda, Santosh, MMIMSR, Geetanjali |
| **600+ beds** | Hamdard, SGT, Integral, Hind |

### 5. Is management quota available in all states?

Yes, all private colleges across states offer management quota with:
- **Higher fees** (20-50% more)
- **Lower cutoffs** (50-100 marks less)
- **Direct college admission** process
