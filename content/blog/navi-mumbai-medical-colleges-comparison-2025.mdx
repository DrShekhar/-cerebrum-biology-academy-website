## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q1: Which college has the best placement/PG success?

**DY Patil** has slightly better NEET PG results historically, but the difference is marginal. Success in NEET PG depends more on individual preparation than college.

| Factor | Impact on PG Success |
|--------|---------------------|
| College | 20% |
| Self-study | 50% |
| Coaching | 20% |
| Clinical Practice | 10% |

### Q2: Is Terna good enough for MBBS?

**Yes.** Terna Medical College:
- Has NMC recognition
- Is affiliated to MUHS
- Produces qualified MBBS doctors
- Alumni successfully clear NEET PG
- The degree is exactly same as any medical college

### Q3: Can I switch from Terna to DY Patil?

**No.** Once admitted:
- No transfer between colleges
- Must complete MBBS where admitted
- Only option: Leave and re-attempt NEET

### Q4: Which has better hostel?

**DY Patil > MGM > Terna**

But hostel quality doesn't impact education quality. Many students stay in PGs near college.

### Q5: Is DY Patil Navi Mumbai same as DY Patil Pune?

**Different institutions:**

| Aspect | DY Patil Pune | DY Patil Navi Mumbai |
|--------|---------------|---------------------|
| Fees | Rs 32L/year | Rs 28L/year |
| Cutoff | 45,000 | 55,000 |
| Hospital | 2,000 beds | 1,800 beds |
| Brand | Slightly higher | Strong |

Both are good, but Pune is considered slightly more prestigious.

### Q6: What about ragging?

All three colleges have **anti-ragging committees** and are generally safe. However:
- DY Patil: Strict monitoring
- MGM: Good control
- Terna: Adequate measures

### Q7: Can I do internship in Mumbai hospitals?

MBBS from any of these colleges allows you to apply for internship at:
- Government hospitals (competitive)
- Private hospitals in Mumbai/Navi Mumbai
- Usually students complete internship at parent hospital
