## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Which is the best medical college among these five?

There's no single "best" - it depends on your priorities:
- **Best Clinical Training**: Subharti
- **Best Infrastructure**: Sharda
- **Best Experience**: Santosh
- **Best Location**: Hamdard
- **Best Modern Campus**: SGT

### 2. Which college has the lowest fees?

**Subharti and Hamdard** both charge â‚¹18.5 Lakhs per year, making them the most affordable among top NCR colleges.

### 3. Which college is best for NEET PG preparation?

**Santosh Medical College** has the best track record for NEET PG, thanks to its experienced faculty and strong clinical training focus.

### 4. Is Sharda worth the extra â‚¹4 Lakhs per year?

If you value:
- Modern infrastructure
- ECFMG approval (US licensing)
- University ecosystem exposure
Then yes, Sharda can be worth the premium.

### 5. How far is Subharti from Delhi practically?

**70 km via NH 58**, typically 1.5-2 hours by car depending on traffic. There are regular bus services from Kashmiri Gate ISBT.

### 6. Which college has the best hostel?

**SGT and Sharda** have the most modern hostel facilities. **Subharti** has the most space. **Hamdard** has limited hostel capacity.

### 7. Can I shift between these colleges after admission?

Generally **no**. Transfers between medical colleges are extremely rare and require exceptional circumstances plus approval from NMC.
