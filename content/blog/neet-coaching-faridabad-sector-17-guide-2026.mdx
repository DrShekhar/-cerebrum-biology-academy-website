# NEET Coaching in Faridabad: The Complete 2026 Guide

Faridabad has one of the largest student populations in Haryana, yet for years, serious NEET aspirants from the city had to commute to South Delhi or Kota for quality coaching. That equation has changed. With a dedicated center in Sector 17 and Violet Line metro connectivity to Delhi, Faridabad students now have access to expert NEET Biology coaching without the exhausting daily commute.

This guide covers everything Faridabad families need — from center location and fee structure to area-wise accessibility and how to fit coaching into a school schedule.

## Why Faridabad for NEET Preparation

### Growing Medical Aspirant Community

Faridabad produces an estimated 8,000-10,000 NEET test-takers annually. The city's large middle-class population, strong school network, and proximity to Delhi create a natural pipeline of medical aspirants. Yet the coaching infrastructure has historically lagged behind the demand.

### Violet Line Metro Connection

The Violet Line metro extension to Faridabad was a turning point. Stations at Bata Chowk, Faridabad Old, and Badkhal Mor connect the city directly to Delhi's coaching ecosystem. Our Sector 17 center is situated near Bata Chowk Metro, making it accessible from across the Faridabad-Delhi corridor.

### Cost of Living Advantage

Compared to Delhi or Gurugram, Faridabad offers significantly lower living costs. Families who live in Faridabad and commute to Delhi for coaching face inflated expenses. A local center eliminates travel costs (estimated ₹3,000-5,000/month for daily Delhi commute) while providing the same quality of instruction.

### DAV Public Schools

Faridabad has multiple DAV branches (Sector 14, NIT, BPTP). DAV students typically have strong NCERT foundations, which is an advantage for NEET preparation. Most DAV students join the evening batch after school.

### Manav Rachna International School

Located in Sector 14, Manav Rachna produces a significant number of NEET aspirants annually. The school's science infrastructure is strong, but students need exam-specific coaching for competitive performance. The Sector 17 center is a 10-minute drive from Manav Rachna.

Apeejay students from the NIT campus are regular NEET aspirants. The school's proximity to our center (within 15 minutes) makes the evening batch particularly convenient.

## Faridabad vs Delhi: Should You Stay Local?

A common question from Faridabad parents: "Should my child travel to Delhi for coaching instead?"

### Reasons to Stay in Faridabad

- **Save 2-3 hours daily** that Delhi commuters lose in travel
- **Lower stress** from avoiding crowded metro during peak hours
- **Same faculty and curriculum** as our Delhi centers
- **Cost savings** of ₹3,000-5,000/month in travel expenses
- **More self-study time** which is where NEET preparation actually happens

### When Delhi Might Make Sense

- If the student lives near the Badarpur border and is equidistant from South Extension
- If the student specifically wants the peer group at a larger Delhi center
- If family already commutes to Delhi daily and the student can join

For most Faridabad families, the local center provides identical quality with significant time and cost savings.
