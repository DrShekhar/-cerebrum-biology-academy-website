# USA & UK Biology-Market Dominance Plan

**Goal:** make Cerebrum the #1 biology coaching institute for **USA and UK** students of **any nationality** (US citizens, UK citizens, locals, expats, and NRIs alike) across **AP Biology, IB Biology (HL/SL), USABO, IBO, BBO, A-Level Biology, GCSE/IGCSE, Brain Bee**, plus the pre-med layer (MCAT, USMLE, DAT, GAMSAT, UCAT).

**Hard guardrails (non-negotiable):**
1. **Additive only** — never edit or restructure existing pages/layouts/components. Every change is a NEW file/route or an opt-in addition.
2. **No thin content, no doorway pages, ever** — every new page is research-verified, genuinely differentiated, and passes the pre-commit SEO/indexability/accuracy checklist (see `feedback-evaluate-pages-before-commit`).
3. **Protect what works** — do not weaken India/NEET SEO or the NRI revenue stream. This plan ADDS a global layer; it does not subtract.
4. **Accuracy** — no fabricated stats/rankings/testimonials. Country claims must survive Google + AI fact-checking.

---

## 1. Strategy in one line
Country-specific **authority hubs** (US, UK) crowning **deep, accurate exam clusters**, each tuned for that country's **SEO + AEO + GEO**, with the brand **translated** out of India-only framing — so a local family in Ohio or Manchester finds us, trusts us, and can convert.

This plan is **5 workstreams** × **2 countries**, delivered in **phased batches**, each batch shippable and evaluated before commit.

## 10. KPIs (how we measure "dominance")
- **Rankings:** top-3 for "AP Biology tutor", "USABO coaching", "Brain Bee coaching" (US); "A-Level Biology tutor", "BBO coaching" (UK), + city long-tails.
- **AEO:** cited by ChatGPT/Perplexity/Google AI for "best [exam] biology tutor [country]".
- **GEO:** indexed, ranking city/school pages per target metro.
- **Funnel:** non-Indian / non-NRI lead share rising (the real goal); US/UK form submissions; trial bookings.
- **GSC:** impressions/clicks from US + UK growing vs the India baseline.
