# Global SEO Domination Plan — Biology Tutor / NEET Biology / NEET Coaching

**Date:** Jul 7, 2026. Basis: 2-agent research (live SERP landscape + 2026 SEO/GEO playbook) synthesized with the June 2026 on-site work (AEO rebuild, entity pages, cannibalization/doorway fixes — DONE).

## Headline findings
1. **We already win where it matters most for AI**: cerebrumbiologyacademy.com ranks **#1 in the Bing-family index** (feeds ChatGPT) for "best NEET biology coaching online" and "best online biology teacher for NEET"; answer engines recite our canonical fact block verbatim. Verify Google.co.in positions in GSC; do NOT restructure these URLs.
2. **The bottleneck is third-party corroboration, not on-site content.** Ahrefs (n=75k brands): brand mentions predict AI visibility 3× better than backlinks (r=0.664 vs 0.218). ~41% of ChatGPT brand recommendations trace to third-party "best-of" listicles. Wikipedia ≈ 48% of ChatGPT top citations. We are absent from all of these.
3. **Winnable vs unwinnable**: US "online biology tutor" head term = DR-80 marketplace wall (don't fight; enter via long-tail + listicle inclusion). UK **A-level biology tutor** = solo tutors with DR<25 on page 1 (most crackable). **IB biology** = tiny EMD sites win (crackable in 6-12mo). India "best NEET coaching" listicle SERP = DR 15-45 mediators we're simply not listed in. Generic "online NEET coaching" (PW/Vedantu) = skip.
4. **Clicks are collapsing on informational queries** (Pew: −47% when AIO shows; #1 CTR down 58%). KPIs must shift to: AI-citation share, branded search volume, review velocity, demo bookings.
5. NEET giants win organic via **free tools** (rank/college predictors — Careers360: 400k students on one tool), **PYQ/notes scale**, **GBP at scale** (PW +220% map impressions), **YouTube** (Competition Wallah 4.5M subs). Teacher-brand queries resolve on YouTube/Quora, not websites.
