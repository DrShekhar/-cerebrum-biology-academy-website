### 1. AI-Powered Learning
- **AI Tutors** - Personalized 1:1 tutoring at scale
- **Adaptive Learning** - Content difficulty adjusts in real-time
- **Automated Assessment** - AI grading with detailed feedback
- **Content Generation** - AI creates practice questions, summaries

**Implication for Cerebrum**: Must have AI-powered doubt solving and personalized recommendations.

### 2. Microlearning & Bite-sized Content
- **Short Videos** - 5-10 minute focused lessons
- **Daily Challenges** - Small daily practice sessions
- **Spaced Repetition** - Intelligent revision scheduling
- **Mobile-First** - Content designed for phone consumption

**Implication for Cerebrum**: Create bite-sized biology content, implement spaced repetition for revision.

### 3. Gamification 2.0
- **XP & Levels** - Progress visualization
- **Streaks** - Daily engagement rewards
- **Leaderboards** - Social competition
- **Achievements** - Milestone celebrations
- **Virtual Currency** - Earn and spend on features

**Implication for Cerebrum**: Already have some gamification, needs enhancement.

### 4. Social Learning
- **Peer Learning** - Students helping students
- **Study Groups** - Virtual collaboration spaces
- **Community Q&A** - Stack Overflow for students
- **Live Sessions** - Real-time interaction with teachers

**Implication for Cerebrum**: Add community features, peer discussion forums.

### 5. Hybrid Learning Models
- **Flipped Classroom** - Video at home, practice in class
- **Blended Learning** - Online + offline integration
- **Cohort-Based** - Learning with a group on schedule

**Implication for Cerebrum**: Support offline centers with online content.
