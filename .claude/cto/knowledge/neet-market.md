## üë• Student Personas

### 1. Class 11 Foundation
**Profile:**
- Age: 15-16 years
- Starting NEET prep early
- 2 years to exam
- Often from educated families

**Needs:**
- Foundation building
- Board exam balance
- Long-term planning
- Conceptual clarity

**Pain Points:**
- Confusion about starting point
- Parent-driven decisions
- School workload balance

**Buying Behavior:**
- Parents make purchase decisions
- Price-sensitive but willing for quality
- Prefer comprehensive packages

---

### 2. Class 12 Intensive
**Profile:**
- Age: 16-17 years
- Main NEET attempt year
- High pressure period
- Board + NEET stress

**Needs:**
- Intensive revision
- Test series
- Quick doubt resolution
- Time management

**Pain Points:**
- Board exam pressure
- Syllabus completion anxiety
- Competition stress

**Buying Behavior:**
- Urgent purchases
- Willing to pay premium
- Multiple resources

---

### 3. Droppers (Repeaters)
**Profile:**
- Age: 17-20 years
- Failed or unsatisfied with score
- Full-time NEET focus
- High motivation (usually)

**Needs:**
- Complete revision
- Weakness identification
- Mental health support
- Practice, practice, practice

**Pain Points:**
- Social stigma
- Depression/anxiety
- Financial pressure
- Time running out feeling

**Buying Behavior:**
- Research thoroughly
- Look for success stories
- Value mentor support
- Price-conscious (already spent a lot)

---

### 4. Partial Droppers
**Profile:**
- College + NEET prep
- Often in BSc or other courses
- Backup plan approach

**Needs:**
- Flexible timing
- Recorded content
- Quick catch-up

**Pain Points:**
- Time management
- Divided focus
- Peer comparison
