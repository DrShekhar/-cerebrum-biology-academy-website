## 1. The three market findings that should drive our decisions

### Finding A — The scholarship test IS the enrollment funnel (and biology-only is empty whitespace)
Every major player acquires students through a branded **scholarship-cum-admission test**, not direct sign-up:

| Test (Brand) | Eligibility | Format | Max discount | Fee | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ANTHE (Aakash) | V–XII + droppers | 60 min MCQ, on/offline | 100% offline / **80% online** | ~₹200–300 | Annual (Oct) |
| TALLENTEX (Allen) | V–X | 2 hr, 80 Q | 90% | ₹300 | Annual (Oct) |
| PWSAT / PWNSAT (PW) | 5/7–12 + droppers | 60 min | 90% / **100%** + cash | **Free** | **Year-round, multi-phase** |
| NSAT (Narayana) | V–X | 2 hr | 90% off / **70% online** | ₹150 | Annual (Oct) |
| MOST (Motion) | 6–12 + droppers | **On-demand, instant result** | 100% (interview-set) | Free | **Always-on** |
| VSAT (Vedantu) | 7–13 + droppers | NEET 90 Q | 100% | ₹99 | Annual |

The universal mechanic: **free/low-fee test → All-India rank → personalized "you won X% off" offer → discount expires unless you enroll by a hard deadline.** Nearly every taker gets *some* slab, so the result becomes a price tag and a warm lead.

**Two gaps no incumbent has closed:**
1. **No player runs a biology-only / biology-weighted branded scholarship test.** Even NEET-exclusive players test full PCB. (Closest: PrepMed "Doctor Hunt" ₹29, still full PCB.)
2. **Generalists reward OFFLINE more than online** (100% offline vs 70–80% online) to pull students into centres. An **online-only** specialist can **invert** this — make online the *top-reward* path.

### Finding B — Biology-only dropper coaching is confirmed white-space, and this cycle is uniquely ours to win
- **Biology = 50% of NEET** (90 questions / 360 of 720 marks; Botany 180 + Zoology 180), the single highest-weight and most NCERT-predictable subject — the lever a dropper uses to add 100+ marks.
- **No national player sells a biology-only dropper.** All bundle full PCB; the only biology-standalone products are ₹399 revision add-ons (e.g. Vedantu TARGET BIO 360). Genuine whitespace for a biology specialist.
- **Credibility angle, this cycle only:** the NEET 2026 leak reportedly involved 120+ matching **Biology** questions. Biology integrity/credibility is unusually salient — a specialist's authority message lands harder now.
- **Droppers need (verified):** diagnostic-first weak-area targeting (not linear re-teach), a test-heavy regimen with deep error analysis, and **mental-health/mentorship support** (a peer-reviewed Chennai study found anxiety symptoms in 100% and depressive symptoms in 59% of NEET-coaching students, rising with attempts). These map directly to small-batch + 1:1 mentorship — our model.
- **Pricing benchmarks:** online full-PCB dropper ≈ ₹5k–₹80k (PW Yakeen 2027 ~₹5.2k/₹7.6k/₹14k tiered; Vedantu ₹3k–₹95k); offline ≈ ₹1.3L–₹2.2L (PW Vidyapeeth disrupts at ~₹40k).
- **Timing:** batches start rolling April–July; expect a **second surge late Jul–Aug 2026** when disappointed re-NEET takers commit to dropping. Our dropper pages already say batches start **25 Jun / 1 Jul / 10 Jul** — well-timed for the first wave; we should add a late-July/Aug "post-result" batch for the second wave.

### Finding C — Class 11 & Foundation are won on the "boards + NEET, started early" pitch — but they are multi-subject by nature
- **Aakash:** Class 8 ₹77k; 2-yr Class 9–10 ₹1.25L; Class 10 ₹80k; online Foundation (9–10) ₹75k; **2-yr integrated Class 11–12 NEET ₹3.5L classroom / ₹1.7L online.** ([propelld](https://propelld.com/site/blog/aakash-institute-fee-structure), [aakashdigital](https://aakashdigital.com/foundation))
- **Allen** runs PNCF (Pre-Nurture Career Foundation) for Class 6–10 (NTSE/Olympiads bundled); **PW Vidyapeeth** is the budget option; PW already sells "NEET from Class 11, 2027–28." ([allen PNCF](https://www.allen.ac.in/pncf/kota/2025-26/pre-nurture-career-foundation-coaching/courses-for-pncf-2025-26.asp), [pw.live class-11](https://www.pw.live/neet/class-11))
- Online NEET coaching spans ₹10.7k–₹3L; offline integrated ₹23k–₹5.4L. ([vedantu](https://www.vedantu.com/blog/jee-neet-coaching-fees-breakdown), [collegedunia](https://collegedunia.com/coaching/neet-institutes))
- **Reality check:** Foundation (8–10) and Class 11 are sold as **all-subject** programs (PCM/B + Mental Ability + boards). A biology-only academy is a *complement*, not a full substitute, here. So the play is **the biology component done best** + the scholarship test as the front door — not pretending to replace a full integrated school.
- **Calendar:** academic session starts April; batches roll April–July; **early-bird discounts run Oct–Feb.** Parents of class-10 leavers decide May–July (post board results).

## 2. Recommendation #1 (highest leverage): launch a Biology Scholarship & Aptitude Test — "Cerebrum Biology Scholarship Test (CBST)"

This is the single biggest opportunity because it (a) is the proven enrollment engine, (b) occupies whitespace nobody owns, and (c) **reuses infrastructure we already have** (the 10,133-question MCQ bank + the test-attempt persistence engine + the CRM upsertLead funnel).

**Design (deliberately inverting the generalists):**
- **Biology-weighted / biology-only** test — our authority subject, the 50% of NEET that matters most.
- **Free, online, on-demand, instant result** (Motion/PW model) — an always-on funnel, not one October spike. Best fit for an online-only academy.
- **Online is the TOP-reward path** (up to 100% on our online biology programs) — the inversion no incumbent offers.
- **Eligibility:** Class 9–12 + droppers (wide net captures foundation→dropper lifecycle).
- **Funnel:** test → All-India biology rank + a free personalized weak-area diagnostic report → scholarship slab on the relevant program → enroll-by-deadline. The diagnostic report doubles as a genuine value-add (droppers specifically want this) and a re-engagement hook.
- **Scholarship slabs:** publish a clean rank→% table (transparency none of the big players offer — a trust differentiator). Suggest 100% / 75% / 50% / 30% / 20% bands.

**Why us / why now:** we already have the question bank and test engine; the test costs us near-zero to run; it turns our biology specialization into the *reason* for the test, not a footnote.

**Decision needed from you:** scholarship slab generosity (how deep a discount we can absorb), and whether CBST is free vs ₹29–99. *Recommendation: free, online-rewarded, on-demand.*

## 4. Recommendation #3: Class 11 NEET + Boards — sell the biology pillar, not a fake full school

- **Don't** pretend to replace a ₹1.7–3.5L integrated school. **Do** offer the **best biology pillar**: "Class 11 Biology for NEET *and* CBSE/state boards together — one specialist, both targets."
- **Structure:** class-11 NCERT biology mapped to *both* board exams and NEET (45 of the 90 NEET bio questions come from class 11), with the same test engine and a clear "boards + NEET, no compromise" message.
- **Two-year track:** Class 11 → Class 12 continuity (the integrated-program logic, biology-only).
- **Pricing:** ~10% below the biology-equivalent share of integrated programs; entry point that the CBST scholarship discounts apply to.
- **Timing:** batches now (April–July session); push the CBST as the entry test.

## 5. Recommendation #4: Foundation (Class 9–10) — a focused biology wedge, scholarship-led

- Smallest-priority of the four, but the **scholarship test naturally feeds it** (generalists use foundation tests to capture students years early).
- Offer a **Class 9–10 Biology Foundation** (NCERT + olympiad + early-NEET concepts) — wedge for serious medical-aspirant families, priced low as a lifecycle entry that later upgrades to Class 11 → Dropper.
- **Don't** over-invest in standalone foundation content; let CBST + a single strong landing page carry it.
