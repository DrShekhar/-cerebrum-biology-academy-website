## Methodology

NEET does not publish country-wise NRI registration data. Demand is estimated from:

1. **Official 14 overseas NEET exam centres** (NTA 2025): Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Doha, Kuwait City, Riyadh, Singapore, Bangkok, Colombo, Kathmandu, Kuala Lumpur, Lagos, Manama, Muscat.
2. **CBSE Gulf Sahodaya membership** (193 principals across GCC = 193 CBSE schools).
3. **CBSE schools abroad count**: UAE 106, Saudi 25+, Kuwait 15–20, Qatar 15–20, Oman 10+, Bahrain 7+, SE Asia + Africa ~30 combined.
4. **Indian diaspora size & student demographics** (MEA, Pew, MOIA data).
5. **Existing NEET/biology coaching supply** (ATP, Allen Overseas, Aakash, TestprepKart presence).

Estimate: **~30,000–40,000 NRI students register for NEET-UG each year globally** (roughly 1.5% of the ~25 lakh total NEET candidates). ~70–80% of those are from GCC.

## Tier A — GCC (Gulf) — primary market

### 🇦🇪 UAE (largest single market)

- **Indian population:** ~3.5 million (largest expat group)
- **CBSE schools:** ~106 (largest cluster outside India)
- **Key school networks:** GEMS (Our Own English High School, DPS Sharjah/Dubai, The Indian High School Dubai — 9,000+ students; the single largest Indian school globally)
- **NEET exam centres:** Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah
- **Cities to target (SEO priority order):**
  1. **Dubai** — heartland. Bur Dubai, Al Quoz, Jumeirah, JVC, Karama, Oud Metha (school clusters)
  2. **Sharjah** — DPS, Our Own, ISMS; dense CBSE footprint
  3. **Abu Dhabi** — Abu Dhabi Indian School (13k+ students), Global Indian International School AD
  4. **Ajman** — overflow from Sharjah
  5. **Al Ain** — smaller but underserved
  6. **Ras Al Khaimah** — small but growing Indian population
- **Competitor density:** HIGH. ATP STEM, Allen Overseas, Aakash UAE, Vibrant.

### 🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia

- **Indian population:** ~2.6 million
- **CBSE schools:** 25+
- **Key schools:** International Indian School Riyadh (IISR, ~5k), IIS Jeddah (~7k), IIS Dammam (~7k)
- **NEET exam centres:** Riyadh (Jeddah/Dammam often available too)
- **Cities to target:**
  1. **Riyadh** — biggest cluster
  2. **Jeddah** — coastal, IIS Jeddah
  3. **Dammam / Al-Khobar** — Eastern Province Indian community
- **Competitor density:** MEDIUM. Aakash Saudi, Allen Overseas.

### 🇶🇦 Qatar

- **Indian population:** ~700,000
- **CBSE schools:** ~15
- **Key schools:** MES Indian School Doha (9k+), DPS Modern Indian School, Bhavan's Qatar, Ideal Indian School
- **NEET exam centre:** Doha
- **Cities to target:** Doha (single metro = whole market)
- **Competitor density:** LOW–MEDIUM. Strong ROI opportunity.

### 🇰🇼 Kuwait

- **Indian population:** ~1 million
- **CBSE schools:** 15–20
- **Key schools:** Indian Community School Kuwait (ICSK, ~12k), Indian Central School, Carmel School, Don Bosco, Bhavan's
- **NEET exam centre:** Kuwait City
- **Cities to target:** Kuwait City, Salmiya, Jabriya
- **Competitor density:** LOW. Underserved → strong SEO opportunity.

### 🇴🇲 Oman

- **Indian population:** ~700,000
- **CBSE schools:** 10+
- **Key schools:** Indian School Muscat (ISM, 7k+), Indian School Al Ghubra, Indian School Salalah, Indian School Sohar (a network of 21 Indian Schools)
- **NEET exam centre:** Muscat
- **Cities to target:** Muscat (primary), Salalah, Sohar, Sur (network presence)
- **Competitor density:** LOW. Underserved.

### 🇧🇭 Bahrain

- **Indian population:** ~350,000
- **CBSE schools:** 7+
- **Key schools:** Indian School Bahrain (12k+; single largest Indian school in Bahrain), New Indian School, Asian School
- **NEET exam centre:** Manama
- **Cities to target:** Manama, Riffa
- **Competitor density:** LOW.

## Tier B — SE Asia + Africa — secondary market

### 🇸🇬 Singapore

- **Indian population:** ~650,000 (9% of residents; long-settled + tech expats)
- **CBSE/ICSE schools:** GIIS Smart Campus + GIIS East Coast, DPS International Singapore, NPS International
- **NEET exam centre:** Singapore
- **Curriculum mix:** CBSE + IB (many Indian-origin kids in IB; still sit NEET for MBBS India)
- **Competitor density:** MEDIUM (mature market, high-end coaching).

### 🇲🇾 Malaysia

- **Indian population:** ~2.9 million (mostly Malaysian Indians, historical diaspora) + Indian expats in KL
- **Key schools:** GIIS KL, Indian International School Malaysia
- **NEET exam centre:** Kuala Lumpur
- **Cities to target:** Kuala Lumpur, Johor Bahru, Penang (smaller expat pockets)
- **Competitor density:** LOW–MEDIUM.

### 🇹🇭 Thailand

- **Indian population:** ~250,000
- **Key schools:** GIIS Bangkok, Modern International School Bangkok (IB/CBSE)
- **NEET exam centre:** Bangkok
- **Cities to target:** Bangkok
- **Competitor density:** LOW.

### 🇳🇬 Nigeria

- **Indian population:** ~50,000 (but very active CBSE ecosystem)
- **Key schools:** Indian Language School Lagos, Delhi Public School Lagos (CBSE)
- **NEET exam centre:** Lagos
- **Cities to target:** Lagos, Abuja (smaller)

### 🇲🇺 Mauritius (no NEET centre)

- **Indian-origin population:** ~850,000 (68% of total population — Indo-Mauritian)
- **Note:** Strong historical ties to Indian medical education; students often take NEET. Low CBSE school count, but consider for content.

### 🇿🇦 South Africa (no NEET centre)

- **Indian-origin population:** ~1.4 million (mostly Durban)
- **Cities:** Durban, Johannesburg
- **Note:** Long-settled community, some interest in MBBS India via NRI quota. Niche.

## Tier C — West (USA, UK, Canada, Australia, NZ) — niche but high-value

Students here mostly study local curriculum (American, British, Canadian, Australian). NEET is a deliberate choice, typically for families that want **NRI quota MBBS in India** (15% quota across Indian medical colleges). Willingness to pay is highest; conversion cycle longer.

### 🇺🇸 USA (no NEET centre — students fly to Dubai/India)

- **Indian-American population:** ~5 million
- **Key metros (Indian-American concentration, in order):**
  1. **New York metro** (including NJ — Edison, Jersey City, Parsippany)
  2. **San Francisco Bay Area** (Fremont, Sunnyvale, Cupertino, San Jose)
  3. **Dallas–Fort Worth** (Irving, Plano, Frisco)
  4. **Houston** (Sugar Land, Pearland)
  5. **Chicago** (Naperville, Schaumburg, Aurora)
  6. **Atlanta** (Johns Creek, Alpharetta)
  7. **Washington DC metro** (Fairfax, Loudoun, Montgomery)
  8. **Los Angeles / Orange County** (Irvine, Cerritos)
  9. **Seattle** (Bellevue, Redmond)
  10. **Boston** (Burlington, Westford)
- **SEO angle:** target "NEET coaching USA", "online NEET prep from [city]", "MBBS India for Indian-Americans", "NRI quota MBBS". Weekend batches (IST evening = US morning).

### 🇬🇧 UK (no NEET centre)

- **Indian-origin population:** ~1.9 million
- **Key cities:** London (Harrow, Southall, Wembley), Leicester, Birmingham, Manchester, Glasgow
- **SEO angle:** "NEET coaching UK", "NRI quota MBBS for UK students", GCSE-to-NEET transition.

### 🇨🇦 Canada (no NEET centre)

- **Indian-origin population:** ~1.8 million
- **Key cities:** Toronto (Brampton, Mississauga, Scarborough), Vancouver (Surrey), Calgary, Edmonton, Montreal
- **SEO angle:** "NEET coaching Canada", Ontario Gr 11-12 to NEET, NRI quota MBBS India.

### 🇦🇺 Australia (no NEET centre)

- **Indian-origin population:** ~780,000 (fastest-growing migrant group)
- **Key cities:** Sydney (Parramatta, Blacktown), Melbourne (Clayton, Wyndham), Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide
- **SEO angle:** "NEET coaching Australia", HSC/VCE to NEET, timezone-aware live classes.

### 🇳🇿 New Zealand (no NEET centre)

- **Indian-origin population:** ~250,000
- **Cities:** Auckland, Wellington, Christchurch
- **SEO angle:** niche content page; low volume but very low competition.
